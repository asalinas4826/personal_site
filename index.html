Welcome!

Thanks for stopping by! My name is Alejandro Salinas and I am a programmer, writer, and musician. This page is for me to put anything I want, including music, short stories, essays, helpful resources and links, and more!

Something else you should know about me: I am a devoted Roman Catholic. My work approaches the world from a traditional Catholic perspective, and I hope to provide resources for Catholics here in the future. I am also a proud groyper and supporter of the America First movement. Christ is King!

Much of this site is a work in progress, so stay tuned!

About Me

I'm a young Catholic man who began to seriously dive into my faith after high school, and found a depth of meaning there that was unlike anything I had ever experienced before. I began to read the Bible, consecrated myself to Mary, and made a lot of edifying Catholic friends. My worldview was radically and forever changed, and now I seek to spread it through means such as this site.

I love to read, usually classics and, much more recently, philosophy. My interest in older literature as well as the Tridentine Mass led me to begin studying Latin in October of 2024. I also speak Spanish, and just began learning Italian.

I use Arch Linux (btw) as my main operating system, with i3 as my window manager. I believe in the Free Software Movement, but I still use a lot of proprietary software. One of these days I'll sit down and work on replacing all of it.

Contact me at asalinas4826@proton.me.
