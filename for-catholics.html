For Catholics

Hello! Here I will post resources, prayers and reflections on the Catholic faith. I have never been formally educated in Catholic teaching as I am not a priest or anything. I try simply to pass on what I read from the saints (and I will cite sources), but don't take anything here too seriously.

A little about my views: I attend the Tridentine Mass and strive for Catholic Orthodoxy on this website and in my personal life. I submit to Pope Leo XIV and everything the Catholic Church professes to be true.
